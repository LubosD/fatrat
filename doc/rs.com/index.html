RapidShare.com support

FatRat's integrated features enable RapidShare.com premium users to download files with ease. And uploads are available to everybody - premium users, collectors and anonymous users. However, bear in mind that RapidShare.com logs uploaders' IP addresses and provides them to whoever demands that their files be deleted!

More features reside in the RapidShare tools window.
